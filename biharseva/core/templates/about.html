Empowering Bihar Through Civic Action

BiharSeva ek aisi pehal hai jo technology aur samudayik sahyog ko jod kar Bihar ke shehron ko swachh aur vyavasthit banane ka kaam karti hai.

Our Mission

Humara maqsad har Bihari nagrik ko jagruk banana hai. Reporting system aur verified volunteer network ke zariye hum kachra-mukti aur behtar traffic management ko haqeeqat banate hain.

Our Vision

Ek aisa Bihar jahan civic sense humari fitrat mein ho. Technology ka upyog karke hum ek transparent aur accountable system banana chahte hain jahan har yogdan ko pehchan (certificate) mile.
