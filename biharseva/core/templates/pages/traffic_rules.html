Live Safety Manual

Road Safety: Zero Tolerance

Bihar ki sadkon par badhti durghatnaon ko rokne ke liye in 4 mukhya niyamon ka sakti se palan karein. Aapki ek galti parivaar par bhari pad sakti hai.

Signal Jumping: Crime

Red light ka matlab hai 'Purn Viram'. Yellow light par rukne ki taiyari karein, na ki raftaar badhayein. Signal todna Bihar Traffic Police ke naye niyamon ke tehat bhari fine aur license cancellation ka karan ban sakta hai.

Fine: Up to ₹5,000 as per New MVA

100% Helmet Usage

Do-pahiya vahan chalak aur piche baithe vyakti (Pillion rider) dono ke liye ISI-certified helmet anivarya hai. Helmet ka strap hamesha lock rakhein. Saste ya plastic helmet suraksha nahi dete.

Mandatory for Pillion Riders in Bihar

Lane Discipline

Zig-zag driving aur galat disha (Wrong side) se overtaking durghatnaon ka sabse bada karan hai. Indicator ka upyog modne se kam se kam 50 metre pehle karein. Heavy vehicles ko rasta dein.

Avoid Blind Spots of Trucks/Buses

Drink & Drive: Jail

Bihar mein sharab-bandi ke tehat nashe mein vahan chalana ek gambhir aparadh hai. Isme na sirf bhari jurmana hai, balki turant giraftari aur jail ka pravdhan bhi hai. Apni aur dusron ki zindagi khatre mein na daalein.

Strict Imprisonment Laws Apply
