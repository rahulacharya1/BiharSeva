How We Clean Bihar

Practical solutions for a sustainable future.

Zero Littering

Bihar ke shehron mein kachra-mukti ke liye hum dustbins install karte hain aur 'Black Spots' (kachra dump points) ko saaf karke wahan plantation karte hain.

Source Segregation

Gila kachra (Wet) aur Sookha kachra (Dry) ko alag karna anivarya hai. Isse landfill par bojh kam hota hai aur recycling plants ki kshamta badhti hai.

Eco-Restoration

Khali padi sarkari zameeno par 'Miyawaki Forest' technique se ghane jungle ugaye ja rahe hain taaki Bihar ka air quality index (AQI) behtar ho sake.
