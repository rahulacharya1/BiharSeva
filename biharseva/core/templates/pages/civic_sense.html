Citizen Responsibility

Civic Sense: The Foundation of a Better Bihar

Civic sense ka matlab sirf safai nahi, balki dusron ke prati samman aur samajik zimmedari hai. Choti aadatein hi Bihar ko mahaan banayengi.

Kachra Dustbin Mein Daalein

Raste par ya khuli jagah par kachra na fenkein. Agar dustbin na mile, toh kachra apne paas rakhein jab tak sahi jagah na mil jaye.

NO OPEN LITTERING

Sarkari Sampatti ki Raksha

Train, Bus, aur Park humari apni sampatti hai. Inhe nuksan na pahunchayein aur na hi deewaron par thookein ya likhein.

PROTECT PUBLIC ASSETS

Shor Kam Karein

Bina wajah horn na bajayein. Raat ke samay loud speakers ka upyog kam karein taaki bimar aur buzugon ko takleef na ho.

NO UNNECESSARY HORN

Katar (Queue) Mein Rahein

Railway station ho ya ration ki dukan, hamesha apni baari ka intezaar karein. Discipline hi ek sabhya samaj ki pehchan hai.

FOLLOW THE LINE

Traffic Rules ka Palan

Red light na todein, helmet pehnein aur hamesha footpath ka istemal karein. Aapki suraksha hi aapke parivaar ki khushi hai.

DRIVE RESPONSIBLY

Madad ke Liye Taiyar

Buzugon, divyangon aur zaroorat-mando ki madad karein. Bihar ki parampara 'Atithi Devo Bhava' aur sahyog par tiki hai.

HELPING COMMUNITY

Civic Sense is not just a habit, it's an Investment in our future.

"Jab hum badlenge, tabhi Bihar badlega."
